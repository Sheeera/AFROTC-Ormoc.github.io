WHY AFROTC?

Air Force is where you'll learn useful life and leadershipskills as you prepare to become an Air Force Officer and earn your college degree at the same time. You'll have people challenging and helping you to accomplish your personal best in academics and physical fitness. You'll develop lifelong friendship and have unique experiences.

Our Story

BASIC CADET

Be an Air Force Basic Cadet & participate in fun activies such as: Rappelling, Water Survival, Weapons Training, Firing, Defense Tactics, Educational Tour, Tree Planting, Civil Military Operations, Leadership.

Our Story

CANDIDATE OFFICER QUALIFYING COURSE

In Air Force Advance Course, you'll make the most of your college experience. You'll hone your time management skills, analytical skills and physical fitness. It won't be easy, but if you're up to the challenge, the rewards will last lifetime. Of course, being an officer have even better privileges: Part time as Security Assistant in EVSU, Scholarship Program, Summer CADRE Training(in BGBNEAB, Mactan, Cebu), CULP(in Guam), Qualified to take POTC, and many more.

AFROTC MISSION AND VISION

Battling an Unseen Enemy, Beyond the Frontlines✈️ ​Through the years, the 220th Airlift Wing of the Philippine Air Force has been known for getting the mission done. In both times of peace and war, with its primary capability of expedited transport of personnel and equipment across the archipelago and overseas, the Wing has always been able to respond during the nation’s most trying periods. All year round - day or night, rain or shine. It delivers noting but quality service with its airlift assets, the C-130s, C-295s, F-27s, C212is and N-22s, cruising through the country and across the globe.
